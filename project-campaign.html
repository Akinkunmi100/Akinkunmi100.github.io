Marketing Campaign Response Prediction

Built a multi-model predictive analytics system to identify customers most likely to respond to marketing campaigns, enabling data-driven targeting strategies and optimizing marketing ROI.

The Challenge

As a data scientist at BangorTelco, I was asked: "Is there a way to determine in advance which customers are likely to accept the offer in the marketing campaign?"

Using data on 20,000+ customers from the company database, the goal was to build predictive models that the sales team could use to target customers likely to accept offers, thereby reducing marketing costs significantly.

  • Predict customer response probability using multiple ML approaches
  • Create interpretable decision rules for the marketing team
  • Discover natural customer groupings for segmentation
  • Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for deployment

The Approach

I developed a comprehensive analytics pipeline using R, combining multiple machine learning approaches to maximize predictive accuracy and business insights:

1. Data Engineering & Cleaning

Extracted data from MySQL database, handled outliers using IQR method, encoded categorical variables (Education, Marital Status), and performed feature engineering.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Analyzed response rates across demographics, created correlation matrices, visualized distributions using ggplot2, and identified key predictive features.

3. Predictive Modeling

Implemented and compared three classification models:

  • Decision Tree (rpart) — Interpretable rules for business stakeholders
  • Logistic Regression — Statistical significance of predictors
  • K-Nearest Neighbors — Non-parametric pattern recognition

4. Customer Segmentation

Applied K-means clustering and hierarchical clustering to identify 3 distinct customer segments based on income, purchasing behavior, and web engagement patterns.

Key Results

~87% Accuracy
0.76 ROC-AUC
3 Customer Segments
2× Targeting Efficiency

Business Impact

  • Targeted Marketing: Identified high-propensity customers, enabling focused campaign efforts
  • Customer Insights: Revealed distinct segments with different purchasing behaviors and demographics
  • Actionable Rules: Decision tree provided interpretable business rules for the marketing team
  • Interactive Dashboard: Power BI visualization for ongoing campaign monitoring and analysis
