Samsung Web Marketing Metrics Analysis

A comprehensive study of Samsung's digital marketing performance (2019-2023), analyzing R&D investment impact, social media effectiveness, and competitive brand positioning using an interactive Excel dashboard.

Project Objectives

In the contemporary era dominated by digital technologies, every organization's expansion and financial success must gauge the efficacy of its marketing endeavours. This study evaluates Samsung's marketing effectiveness through key performance indicators.

  • Examine Samsung's product performance and innovation
  • Investigate Samsung's market trends over 5 years
  • Assess Samsung's overall market performance vs competitors

Executive Summary

Through the dataset obtained from different sources, calculations, pivot tables, and charts were explored to create an interactive dashboard for Samsung.

$54M+ Total R&D Investment
+34% ROI Increase
82% Smartphone Market Share
+19.5% Profit Margin Growth

The consistent investment in research and development from 2021 to Q1 2023 boosted the organization's return on investment by 34% and their profit margin by 19.5%. Effective investment in R&D has put Samsung in the top 5 of smartphone production, with 82% market share in the smartphone business.

Key Findings

Product Performance & Innovation

Consistent R&D investment resulted in 82% smartphone market share. Samsung ranks among the top 5 smartphone producers globally. Notable innovations include Galaxy Z Flip and Galaxy Fold.

Market Trends

Revenue increased from $51,684 (2019) to $95,926 (2022) — an 85% growth. Brand value increased by 12.1% compared to competitors like Apple, placing Samsung in the top 10 global brands for 2023.

Social Media Marketing Effectiveness

Strategic social media presence boosted engagement: +9% clicks, +4% impressions, and -2% cost per impression — demonstrating high campaign efficiency.

Competitive Position

Despite stiff competition from Apple, Samsung maneuvered through significant investment in R&D and social media marketing. Samsung smartphones dominated market share ahead of Xiaomi, Vivo, Apple, and Huawei.

Metrics Analyzed

Financial & Performance Metrics

  • Market Share — Proportion of sales in smartphone, TV, and tablet markets
  • ROI — Campaign effectiveness (revenue vs costs)
  • R&D Investment — Research contribution to market positioning
  • Brand Value — Market perception of brand strength
  • Profit Margin — Revenue to profit efficiency
  • Unit Sales — Device quantity for pricing optimization

Social Media Metrics

  • Clicks — User interactions with Samsung ads
  • Impressions — Ad display frequency on screens
  • Cost Per Impression — Expenditure per 1,000 impressions

Strategic Recommendations

1 Television Market Focus: Increase R&D investment in the television segment and track unit sales more closely
2 Customer Satisfaction: Explore customer satisfaction metrics to further improve brand value
3 Supply Chain Data: Collect more data on supply chain efficiency for better customer perception insights
4 Competitive Strategy: Revamp marketing campaigns (2003-2008 strategy increased brand value by 200%)
