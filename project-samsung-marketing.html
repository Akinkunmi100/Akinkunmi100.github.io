Samsung Marketing Metrics Dashboard

Built an interactive Excel dashboard to track Samsung's web marketing performance, analyzing revenue trends, market share, ROI, and brand value metrics across 2019-2023.

The Challenge

Samsung needed a comprehensive view of their web marketing performance across multiple metrics and years. The challenge was to consolidate disparate data sources into a single, interactive dashboard that executives could use for strategic decision-making.

Key Requirements

  • Track revenue growth year-over-year (2019-2023)
  • Visualize market share by product category
  • Monitor ROI and R&D investment trends
  • Compare brand value against competitors
  • Analyze cost per impression and profit margins

The Solution

I designed an interactive Excel dashboard with multiple visualization components:

1. Revenue KPI Cards

Year-over-year revenue with percentage growth indicators showing progression from $51K (2019) to $96K (2022) — a 33% growth rate.

2. Market Share Breakdown

Donut chart showing product distribution: Smartphones (213K units), Television (37K), Tablets (8.6K) with 63% smartphone dominance.

3. Performance Trends

Line charts for R&D Investment, ROI, Profit Margin, and Impressions with monthly granularity and year-over-year comparisons.

4. Competitive Brand Analysis

Horizontal bar chart comparing Samsung's brand value against Amazon, Apple, Google, Microsoft, and other major competitors.

Key Insights

33% Revenue Growth
$96K Peak Revenue
63% Smartphone Share
5 Years Data Range

Dashboard Features

  • Interactive Filters: Month and Year slicers for dynamic data exploration
  • Trend Analysis: Multi-year comparison across all key metrics
  • Growth Indicators: Automatic percentage change calculations
  • Brand-Aligned Design: Samsung blue color scheme for professional presentation
