Consumer Behavior Analysis

Published comprehensive mixed-methods research on digital purchasing patterns, directly influencing the UX redesign of a fintech app.

The Hypothesis

The study aimed to understand why 30% of users dropped off during the final checkout stage. We hypothesized that trust markers and transparent pricing models were the missing variables in the user journey.

Methodology

Adopted a mixed-methods approach involving survey data (n=1,200) and behavioral analysis of app logs.

  • Performed Cluster Analysis using R to identify user segments.
  • Conducted A/B testing on pricing display formats.
  • Utilized Regression Analysis to pinpoint key factors driving conversion.
