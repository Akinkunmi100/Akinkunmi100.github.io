# 📊 Samsung Marketing Metrics Dashboard

**Interactive Excel Dashboard for Web Marketing Analytics**

---

## 🎯 Overview

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to track Samsung's web marketing performance metrics, enabling data-driven strategic decision-making across multiple product categories and years.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

| Metric | 2019 | 2020 | 2021 | 2022 | Growth |
|--------|------|------|------|------|--------|
| **Revenue** | $51,684 | $62,021 | $82,665 | $95,926 | **33%** |

### Market Share by Product
- **Smartphones**: 213,246 units (63%)
- **Television**: 37,211 units
- **Tablets**: 8,658 units

---

## 🛠️ Dashboard Components

### 1. Revenue KPI Cards
Year-over-year revenue with automatic percentage growth indicators

### 2. Market Share Donut Chart
Visual breakdown of product distribution with dominant smartphone segment

### 3. Trend Line Charts
- R&D Investment over time
- Return on Investment (ROI)
- Profit Margin monthly trends
- Impressions performance

### 4. Cost Per Impression Analysis
Monthly CPI breakdown by channel

### 5. Brand Value Comparison
Horizontal bar chart comparing Samsung against:
- Amazon
- Apple
- Google
- Microsoft
- Walmart
- Samsung Group
- ICBC

### 6. Performance Metrics
- Clicks by month
- Unit Sales trends
- Impressions over time

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Features

- **Month Filter**: Slice data by specific months (Jan-Dec)
- **Year Slicer**: Filter across 2019-2023
- **Dynamic Charts**: All visualizations update based on filter selections
